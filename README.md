## Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The objective was to develop essential digital skills such as creating professional online profiles, understanding safe internet practices, using collaboration tools, and practicing effective digital communication. The project consists of five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy.

---

## Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
An infographic was created using Canva to explain key concepts of digital literacy, including safe internet practices, useful digital tools, professional online presence, and email etiquette. The aim was to present information in a clear and visually engaging format.

---

## Task 2: Digital Portfolio
Profiles were created on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to establish a professional online presence. These platforms will be used to showcase projects, build a network, and improve technical skills over time.

---

## Task 3: Coding and Collaboration Platforms
A beginner-level coding problem was solved using HackerRank to understand basic programming concepts. Additionally, a Google Form titled "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz" was created to assess students’ understanding. The form includes both multiple-choice and short-answer questions.

## Task 4: Email Etiquette
Two professional emails were drafted: one requesting an assignment extension and another expressing interest in an internship. A checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible social media usage was also created.

---

## Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
A case study on UPI fraud was prepared, explaining how such scams occur and their impact. A prevention checklist was also created to promote safe online practices among students.

---

## Conclusion
This project helped in understanding the importance of digital literacy and developing practical skills required for academic and professional growth. It also improved awareness of online safety and responsible digital behavior.
