SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS

DO's:
1. Keep your profile professional and clean
2. Think carefully before posting content
3. Use privacy settings to control your information
4. Share useful and positive content
5. Respect others and maintain polite communication

DON'Ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information
2. Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
3. Do not engage in online arguments or conflicts
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
5. Do not spread misinformation or unverified content
