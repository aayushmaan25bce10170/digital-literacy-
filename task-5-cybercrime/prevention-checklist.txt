STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1. Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
2. Do not approve unknown or suspicious UPI payment requests
3. Remember that receiving money does not require approval
4. Avoid clicking on unknown links or messages
5. Always verify the identity of the sender before making payments
6. Use only official and trusted applications for transactions
7. Enable app locks and two-factor authentication for security
8. Regularly check your bank account for any unusual activity
9. Do not scan random QR codes from unknown sources
10. Immediately report fraud on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
